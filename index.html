About Us

Pure Zen Essence, a family-owned natural products business proudly rooted in the heart of Wakefield Savannah, St. Catherine, Jamaica. Founded by the Carty family, our mission is to provide 100% Natural products that promote overall health and wellness.

At Pure Zen Essence, we believe that nature holds the key to true balance and vitality. One of our flagship products is cold-pressed castor oil, celebrated for its nourishing properties for hair, skin, and overall wellness. Every product we create is thoughtfully crafted to enhance your well-being while remaining environmentally conscious.
